Source and Import Notes

This is the Summa Theologica of St. Thomas Aquinas, translated by the Fathers of the English Dominican Province [1947].

Source: Internet Sacred Text Archive. Sacred Texts identifies this translation as public domain in the United States.

The source HTML was retrieved on July 19, 2026 primarily from preserved Sacred Texts pages at the Internet Archive.
